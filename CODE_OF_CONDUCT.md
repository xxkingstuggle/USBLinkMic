# Code of Conduct

USB LinkMic follows a simple rule: be respectful, constructive, and specific.

We welcome bug reports, feature ideas, documentation fixes, and experiments. We do not accept harassment, personal attacks, or intentionally disruptive behavior.

Maintainers may close or remove conversations that are abusive, off-topic, or unsafe.

---

# 行为准则

USB LinkMic 遵循一个简单原则：尊重他人、建设性沟通、描述具体问题。

欢迎提交 bug、功能建议、文档修正和实验性想法。不接受骚扰、人身攻击或故意破坏讨论秩序的行为。

维护者可以关闭或删除带有攻击性、偏离主题或不安全的讨论。
